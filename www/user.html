Upgrade

To upgrade from earlier versions of Hudson, simply redeploy the WAR file. In case of Glassfish, this means either just overwriting your $GLASSFISH/domains/domain1/autodeploy/hudson.war, or perform another deployment through the admin GUI (http://localhost:4848/ on mosy systems.) In case of Tomcat, this means just overwriting your $TOMCAT/webapps/hudson.war and restarting Tomcat (or see Tomcat documentation for other ways to redeploy.)
