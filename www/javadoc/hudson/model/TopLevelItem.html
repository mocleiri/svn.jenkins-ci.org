public interface TopLevelItem
extends Item, ExtensionPoint, Describable<TopLevelItem>

Item that can be directly displayed under Hudson.

To register a custom TopLevelItem class from a plugin, put Extension on your TopLevelItemDescriptor. Also see Items.XSTREAM.
