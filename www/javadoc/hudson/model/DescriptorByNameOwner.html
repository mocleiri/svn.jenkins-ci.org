public interface DescriptorByNameOwner
extends ModelObject

Adds getDescriptorByName(String) to bind Descriptors to URL. Binding them at some specific object (instead of Hudson), allows Descriptors to perform context-specific form field validation.

Descriptor.getCheckUrl(String) finds an ancestor with this interface and generates URLs against it.
